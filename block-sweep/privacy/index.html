Privacy Policy — Block Sweep: Fly & Clear

Effective: July 19, 2026

The short version

Block Sweep has no accounts, no sign-in, no first-party analytics and no server of our own — your game runs on your device. The app shows ads through Google AdMob (one passive banner and one optional rewarded ad you can watch to revive a run). All ads are non-personalized: every ad request is sent with the non-personalized-ads flag (npa=1), the app never uses the advertising identifier (IDFA) for tracking, and it never asks for tracking permission because it does not track you.

What stays on your device

Your highscores, settings and game progress are saved locally on your device only. Deleting the app deletes this data. We cannot access it.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

To serve and measure non-personalized ads and to prevent fraud, Google's advertising SDK may collect device information such as a device identifier, coarse location derived from your IP address, ad interaction data (which ads were shown or tapped), and diagnostic data (crash logs, launch time). This data is not used to track you across other companies' apps or websites and is not linked to your identity by us. Google processes this data under its own privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy. More on how Google uses data from apps that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Purchases

Block Sweep contains no in-app purchases.

Children

Block Sweep is a general-audience game rated 4+. Because all ads are non-personalized and the app collects no personal data itself, no profile of any player — child or adult — is built by us.

Your choices

Ads in Block Sweep are always non-personalized, so there is no ad-personalization setting to manage. You can limit ad tracking device-wide in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.

Future changes

If a future update changes what data is collected, this policy and the App Store privacy label will be updated first.
