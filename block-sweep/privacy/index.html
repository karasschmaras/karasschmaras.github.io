Privacy Policy — Block Sweep: Fly & Clear

Effective: July 19, 2026

The short version

Block Sweep does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no server — the game runs entirely on your device.

What stays on your device

Your highscores, settings and game progress are saved locally on your device only. Deleting the app deletes this data. We cannot access it.

Purchases

Block Sweep currently contains no in-app purchases.

Future changes

If a future update adds advertising or online features, this policy and the App Store privacy label will be updated first, and any such features will follow Apple's privacy rules including consent where required.
