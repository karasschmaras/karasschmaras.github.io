Privacy Policy — Sticker Transfer

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Sticker Transfer ("the app", "we", "us") is a free app published by Leon Karas trading as Karas Games (Germany). This policy explains what data the app collects and why. We keep it short and honest.

The short version

  • Your photos and screenshots never leave your device. Sticker Transfer turns your own screenshots, photos, and videos into WhatsApp stickers entirely on your device. There is no account, no login, no server of ours, and no cloud upload of your images. We never see, store, or transmit the pictures you import or the stickers you make.
  • The app is free and ad-supported. To show ads we use Google AdMob. AdMob collects some data to serve and measure those ads. That is the only data collection in the app.
  • A one-time in-app purchase, "Sticker Transfer Pro", removes all ads. Purchases are handled by Apple / Google — we never see your payment details.
  • The rest is detail.

1. Who we are (data controller)

The data controller responsible for this app is:

Leon Karas (Karas Games) — Germany
Contact for any privacy question: karasleon@web.de

2. What stays on your device (not collected by us)

Everything about making a sticker happens locally on your device. Specifically:

  • Photo library access (Apple PhotoKit). With your permission, the app reads screenshots, photos, or videos you choose so it can turn them into stickers. We fully support iOS limited-photo-access — you can grant access to selected items only, and the app never forces full-library access. This access is used only on your device to create stickers; nothing from your photo library is uploaded or sent to us.
  • On-device image processing. Subject detection and background removal use Apple Vision on your device; WebP encoding uses the on-device libwebp library. No image is sent anywhere for processing.
  • Sticker packs and settings are stored locally on your device (Apple SwiftData / on-device storage).
  • Handover to WhatsApp. When you tap "Add to WhatsApp", the finished sticker pack is passed to WhatsApp through the system share/pasteboard mechanism on your device. From that point the sticker is inside WhatsApp and is governed by WhatsApp's own privacy policy. We do not transmit it and are not involved in that transfer beyond handing it to the app you chose.

None of the above is collected, stored, or seen by us. It lives on your device and is removed when you delete the app.

3. Data collected by Google AdMob (advertising)

Sticker Transfer is free and supported by ads: discreet banner ads in the gallery / pack-management screens (never during import or the editor), and an optional rewarded video you may choose to watch when exporting a pack (your very first export is always ad-free). Ads are served by Google AdMob (the Google Mobile Ads SDK), which is the app's only third-party SDK.

Our app's own privacy manifest declares that the following data is collected and used to track you across apps and websites:

As part of serving and measuring ads, Google AdMob and its partners may additionally process your approximate (coarse) location derived from your IP address and crash / performance diagnostics, as described in Google's own disclosures. We (Karas Games) do not receive, store, or link any of this data to your identity, and we operate no database of our own.

Because the app uses tracking for personalized advertising, our privacy manifest sets tracking = enabled and lists Google's advertising domains (googleadservices.com, google-analytics.com, googlesyndication.com, doubleclick.net).

How Google handles this data is governed by Google's own policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses information from apps that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

4. In-app purchase (Sticker Transfer Pro)

The app offers one optional in-app purchase: a one-time, non-consumable "Sticker Transfer Pro" that removes all advertising permanently. The purchase is processed by Apple (StoreKit) or by Google (Google Play Billing). We never see or receive your payment-card details. Your Pro entitlement is managed by the app store and confirmed on your device; use the in-app Restore Purchases control to re-confirm it on a new or reinstalled device (this asks the app store — not us — to re-check your entitlement). There is no subscription in this version.

5. Your choices: tracking & consent

  • App Tracking Transparency (ATT, iOS). Before any tracking-related identifier is used, iOS asks for your permission. You can decline. You can change your choice any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • EU/EEA/UK consent (GDPR). If you are in the EU/EEA or UK, a Google-certified consent message (Google's User Messaging Platform, "UMP") is shown before personalized ads are used. You can review or withdraw your consent at any time via the in-app privacy-choices / consent option.
  • Declining tracking or consent does not limit the app — you can still create and export stickers; you simply see non-personalized ads instead of personalized ones.

6. Legal bases for processing (GDPR)

  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — for personalized advertising and the associated use of a device/advertising identifier and tracking, based on your ATT permission and the EU/EEA/UK consent screen. You may withdraw consent at any time (see §5); withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — for processing an in-app purchase you request (handled by the app store).
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — for the limited, non-tracking processing needed to run and secure the ads SDK (e.g. showing non-personalized ads and basic diagnostics) where you have not consented to personalization.

7. Third parties we share data with

  • Google (AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK) — for serving and measuring ads, as described in §3. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy).
  • Apple / Google (app stores) — for processing your in-app purchase (§4). Governed by Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.
  • WhatsApp — only the finished sticker pack you choose to export is handed to WhatsApp on your device (§2), after which it is governed by WhatsApp's privacy policy.

We do not sell your personal data, and we have no other third-party analytics, crash, or attribution SDKs.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you have rights over your personal data. Because the only personal data the app collects is collected and controlled by Google for advertising, the most direct controls are:

  • Google Ads Settings: adssettings.google.com
  • The EU/EEA/UK consent screen and the in-app withdraw-consent option (§5), and ATT in iOS Settings.
  • The Google Privacy Policy controls: policies.google.com/privacy

Under the GDPR / UK GDPR, you have the rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and object to processing of your personal data, and to data portability. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

Under the CCPA/CPRA (California), we do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising beyond the personalized-advertising choice you control via ATT and the consent screen; you may exercise a "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" choice by declining tracking (ATT) and withholding/withdrawing consent, which makes all ads non-personalized.

For any request or question, contact karasleon@web.de. Note: because we hold no personal data of our own (we run no server and keep no database), for the ad data itself we will direct you to Google's controls above, which is where that data actually resides.

9. Children's privacy

Sticker Transfer is a general-audience app. It is not directed to children, is not offered in a "kids" category, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children or target advertising to them. Because the app shows third-party ads, it is intended for a general audience. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, contact us at karasleon@web.de and we will help. This is consistent with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent children's-privacy rules.

10. International data transfers

Such transfers are governed by Google's safeguards (e.g. EU Standard Contractual Clauses) under the Google Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy). The images, stickers, and packs on your device are not transmitted and are not subject to any transfer.

11. Data retention

We retain no personal data on our own systems (we have none). Ad-related data is retained by Google under its own policies. Your stickers, packs, and settings remain on your device until you delete them or delete the app.

2. Was auf deinem Gerät bleibt (von uns nicht erhoben)

Alles rund um die Sticker-Erstellung passiert lokal auf deinem Gerät. Konkret:

  • Zugriff auf die Fotobibliothek (Apple PhotoKit). Mit deiner Erlaubnis liest die App die von dir gewählten Screenshots, Fotos oder Videos, um daraus Sticker zu machen. Wir unterstützen den iOS-„eingeschränkten Fotozugriff" vollständig – du kannst den Zugriff auf einzelne Objekte beschränken, und die App erzwingt niemals den Vollzugriff. Dieser Zugriff dient ausschließlich lokal der Sticker-Erstellung; nichts aus deiner Fotobibliothek wird hochgeladen oder an uns gesendet.
  • Bildverarbeitung auf dem Gerät. Motiverkennung und Freistellung nutzen Apple Vision auf deinem Gerät; die WebP-Kodierung nutzt die lokale libwebp-Bibliothek. Kein Bild wird zur Verarbeitung irgendwohin gesendet.
  • Sticker-Packs und Einstellungen werden lokal auf deinem Gerät gespeichert (Apple SwiftData / Gerätespeicher).
  • Übergabe an WhatsApp. Wenn du auf „Zu WhatsApp hinzufügen" tippst, wird das fertige Sticker-Pack über den System-Teilen-/Zwischenablage-Mechanismus deines Geräts an WhatsApp übergeben. Ab diesem Zeitpunkt liegt der Sticker in WhatsApp und unterliegt WhatsApps eigener Datenschutzerklärung. Wir übertragen ihn nicht und sind an diesem Vorgang nur insoweit beteiligt, als wir ihn der von dir gewählten App übergeben.

Nichts davon wird von uns erhoben, gespeichert oder eingesehen. Es bleibt auf deinem Gerät und wird beim Löschen der App entfernt.
