How it works

  • Screenshot a sticker you like (or import any photo).
  • Sticker Transfer finds it and cuts it out automatically.
  • Batch-select a whole grid at once, add emoji tags, and send the pack straight to WhatsApp.
  • 100% on-device — your photos never leave your phone, no account.

Frequently asked

Is it free? Yes — free with ads. A one-time Sticker Transfer Pro purchase removes all ads forever (no subscription). Use Restore Purchases in Settings to re-activate it on a new device.

Where do my photos go? Nowhere. All processing happens on your device; we run no server and keep no account.

My sticker won't add to WhatsApp. Make sure WhatsApp is installed and up to date, then try exporting the pack again. Still stuck? Email us above.
