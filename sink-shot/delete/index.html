Delete your Sink Shot account

Sink Shot uses an anonymous account (no e-mail, no phone number). You can delete it and all associated data at any time:

In the app (instant):

Settings → Account → Delete account & data → confirm.
This forfeits your open matches, deletes your player record, display name, push tokens and the anonymous account itself on our servers, and wipes all local data on your device.

Without the app:

E-mail karasleon@web.de from any address and include your in-game display name; we delete the matching anonymous account's data within 30 days.

What is deleted: your player record (display name, notification tokens), matchmaking tickets, open invites, and your side of match participation (finished match records are anonymized). Purchases are managed by Apple/Google and can be restored on a new account only where the store supports it.
